Why PeerCaaS?

Leverage the power of decentralized infrastructure for faster, cheaper, and more resilient deployments.

P2P Container Network

Direct peer-to-peer connections between containers using WebRTC and relay fallback for maximum throughput and resilience.

Earn Rewards

Contribute idle computing resources as a worker node and earn rewards. The more you share, the more you earn.

Ultra-Low Latency

Smart matchmaking algorithm places your containers on the nearest available worker, minimizing network hops and latency.

How It Works

Get your containers running in three simple steps.

1

Deploy

Choose your Docker image and configure ports, env variables. Use our form or paste a docker run command.

2

Match

Our matchmaking engine finds the optimal worker node based on proximity, capacity, and network conditions.

3

Connect

Your container goes live with a direct P2P tunnel. Monitor traffic, manage instances, and scale on demand.
